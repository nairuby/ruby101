Maps

  • Typically, goods need to be delivered to customers. Thus, a customer needs to indicate where they want the goods delivered to. Maps provide a simple way to indicate a location. There are a number of map service providers. Some include:
    • Baidu maps
    • E-charts maps
    • Google maps
    • Here maps
    • Mapbox
    • Open street map
    • Tom tom
    • Yandex maps
